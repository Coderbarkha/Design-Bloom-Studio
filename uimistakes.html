5 UI Mistakes That Are Killing Your Conversions

Simple design changes that increase clarity and conversions.
By Design Bloom • Dec 8, 2025

Many websites lose customers because of small UX/UI mistakes. Fix these first:

  1. Unclear CTA: If users can't tell what action to take, conversion drops. Use one primary CTA above the fold.
  2. Slow load: Heavy images slow pages. Compress images and use proper dimensions.
  3. Poor hierarchy: Make headings distinct — users scan pages, not read them.
  4. Mobile-unfriendly forms: Use single-column forms and remove unnecessary fields.
  5. Distracting visuals: Avoid too many competing animations or loud colors around CTAs.

Start with analytics: identify the highest-traffic landing page, apply one change, measure conversion for 7–14 days.
